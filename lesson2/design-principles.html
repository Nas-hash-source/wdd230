Alignment

BYU Idaho

When you look at the BYU Idaho canvas' website, it does not disturb our eyes because the boxes that contain the three buttons are aligned. Human's psychology tends to organize things which makes them comfortable to look at it.

Repetition

Openclassrooms

For the openclassrooms'website, there are alignments as well but I would like to highlight the repetition of the patterns such as the oval shape that fitting the pictures and the design of the paragraph that follows with it. The user loves something that are similars.

White space

Duolingo

The white space help the users relax a little bit because it looks clean
