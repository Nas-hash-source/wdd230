Target Audience description

Personas

Persona #1 : Entrepreuner becomer

  • Fictional Name : Karen Andria
  • Demographics :
    • has a job
    • 30 years old
    • Married and mother of a child
    • would like to build his own business
    • has a business knowledge background
  • Goals :
    • looks for trainings and counsellings on how to build a business
    • knows in general about the services that the chamber of commerce offers
    • studying statistics and surveys to make a good decision on launching her businesses.
  • Other information:
    • possesses a capital to invest or represents someone who wants to funds a project.
    • has already searched about the business creation process
    • is intermediate Internet user

Persona #2 : Entrepreuner

  • Fictional Name : Tojo Rabe
  • Demographics :
    • CEO of a Company
    • 50 years old
    • Married and father of three children
    • Is experimented in business
  • Goals :
    • searches for statistics, surveys and informations on his industry
    • connects with the Chamber of commerce members
    • interested in market updates and the activities of the Chamber

Scenarios

Scenario #01

A Marketing Assistant was assigned to conceive questions about a survey which is crucial to decide on which potential clients his company targets first. So, he ended up asking some information such as statistics of the young people in Madagascar from the Chamber.

Scenario #02

Jane Anderson would like to establish her first company, she wants to know how to create a business plan. She decided to check the Chamber's trainings if they can solve her problem or even more.

Scenario #03

A CEO of several companies once saw that the Chamber of the commerce published a public market on facebook, he realized that some of those markets may be profitable for the development of his companies. So, he decides to check the website often to keep updated with them.

Scenario #04

A member of the Chambers would like to partner with other companies, he would like to request for counselings from them because they may have informations about others' business.
